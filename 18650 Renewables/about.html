About 18650 Renewables

We are a team of young passionate individuals who are passionate about bringing sustainable energy to the 1.3 billion people who lack access globally and also help mitigate green house gas emissions in the process, with a special focus on the African Continent which has a young rapidly growing population and a rising demand for reliable energy.
Our vision is to positively impact the lives of people globally. We are achieving this by continuously innovating our solutions and capabilities, we believe sustainable energy access is one of the pillars of sustainable human development
