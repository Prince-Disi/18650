RENEWABLE ENERGY

Sub-Saharan Africa is in the middle of a power crisis, with large portions of the population not having access to electricity. Businesses and consumers who do have access to the grid often have to deal with daily power outages which can last up to several days.
Most businesses across Sub-Saharan Africa rely on expensive and polluting diesel generators to bridge the supply-demand gap of electricity. 18650 Renewables focuses on small- to mid-sized hybrid solar power solutions for commercial and residential customers.

OUR SERVICES

RESIDENTIAL SOLAR PV HYBIRD SOLUTIONS

We provide our customers with a solar pv hybrid system designed to match their energy needs and consumption. Customers can mitigate the need for burning diseal/petrol whenever they need baseload power.

COMMERCIAL SOLAR PV HYBRID SOLUTIONS

We provide our commercial customers with a turnkey solar pv hybrid system designed to match their energy needs and consumption, with the added benefit of redcuing OPEX and also their carbon footprint.

HOW IT WORKS

AUDIT

We conduct an in-depth audit of the site, taking all electricity consumers into account.

PROPOSE

In a detailed proposal, we explain by how much we will reduce electricity expenses and how the system works.

INSTALL

Our in-house installation team will install the system, in compliance with best-practice safety and quality standards

OPERATION

We operate and maintain the system, ensuring that it delivers continuous and reliable power.
