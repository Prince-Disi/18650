SECOND LIFE STORAGE

According to research carried out by the University of Oxford, most laptop batteries are discarded with at least 60% of capacity still left in them.
With the global stockpile of used EV batteries expected to exceed 3.4 milion packs by 2025 there is a tremendous potential for reuse in less demanding applications other than electric drivetrains like residential energy storage or solar streetlight systems rather than being discarded in landfills. At 18650 Renewables we harvest the useful lithium ion cells from the battery packs and recombine them into useful small scale energy storage devices.

HOW WE DO IT

Collect

Discarded battery packs are collected from our partners in our growing supply chain network

Test

Each indivdual cell from each pack is tested for remaining capacity and stability using our specilized testing equipment

Sort

Each indivdual cell is sorted and grouped into packs of similar capacities

Reuse

Cells are reused for different small scale energy storage devices like powerbanks, led lighting kits, dc power box, powerwalls etc.
